# Evony: The King's Return — Combat & Battle Mechanics

## Troop classes (4)
- **Ground / Infantry** and **Mounted / Cavalry** — melee, engage at close range.
- **Ranged / Archers** and **Siege Machines** — ranged, strike from a distance.

## Counter system (rock-paper-scissors)
- **Ranged beat Mounted** ("range outpower horses")
- **Mounted beat Ground** ("horses outpower ground")
- **Ground beat Ranged** ("ground counters archers")
- **Siege beat Ranged** (siege counters archers, but siege is fragile / low HP)

## Tiers T1–T15
- Each class has tiers; higher tier = more Attack/Defense/HP but costlier and slower to train, unlocked by Barracks/Academy level.
- **T1 is cheapest, fastest, weakest** — trained in enormous numbers as a **meat-shield / layer**. This is the tier the bot mass-produces.

## Layering (core PvP concept)
- Combat runs in hidden **rounds**; each troop type at each tier targets ONE enemy group per round.
- **Thick defensive layers** protect a city; **thin offensive layers** ride along with attack marches.
- Filling a march/defense with many tiers forces the enemy's strong troops to waste rounds chewing through each layer. Example: 90% top-tier archers + thin layers of every other tier makes the enemy's top archers waste ~51 rounds on filler.

## Stats & stacking buffs
- Base per-troop **Attack, Defense, HP**, multiplied by stacking buffs from: monarch/**generals**, **Keep**, **subordinate cities**, **blazons**, **spiritual beasts/dragons**, **Academy tech**, and situational **PvP attack/def/HP** and **monster-attack** buffs.

## March, rally, reinforcement
- **March size** caps troops per march (raised by generals/tech/VIP).
- **Rally** = combine alliance troops into one large hit on a boss or enemy city.
- **Reinforcement / garrison** = send troops to defend an ally or hold a city.

## Wounded vs death, hospital
- On **defense** (with Wall + Hospital), losses become **wounded** up to hospital capacity and can be healed; overflow **die**.
- On **attack**, losses skew toward death.
- Good layering absorbs damage so higher-tier troops survive.

## PvP vs PvE
- **PvP**: attack players, "zero" a city, use peace shields / bubbles / truce to avoid being hit.
- **PvE**: monsters, bosses, and boss events.

## Server wars
- **SvS / Battlefield / KvK** cross-server competition events.

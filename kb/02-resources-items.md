# Evony: The King's Return — Resources, Economy & Resource Items

## Resource types
- **Food, Lumber, Ore, Iron, Gold**, plus premium **Gems**. Produced by resource buildings, gathered on world-map tiles, granted by items, and shared via alliance help.

## Resource items (item bag)
- Resources also exist as **items** in the bag, in fixed **denominations** (e.g. 1K, 10K, 100K, 1M, 10M, 100M, 1B ...). Using/opening one credits that amount to your stores.
- Opening can **double** the yield with a chance buff.
- **Best practice: keep resources as items until you need them.** Items in the bag cannot be **looted** on defeat and are not eaten by troop **upkeep**. Only open them right before spending (training/building).

## "Top up food 1B at a time" (the automatable task)
- Food items come in fixed denominations; the largest common food pack is opened **one tap at a time** (~1B food per tap). To fund very large training you open many 1B food items in sequence — hence "top up 1B at a time."
- This repetitive open-item action is exactly what a bot can automate: **when food < one training batch, open N x 1B food items from the bag, then resume training.** (Planned next feature — see 05-bot-project.md.)

## Upkeep / negative food
- Troops carry **food upkeep**; when consumption outpaces production, stored food can show as **negative (red)**. In TKR troops do **not** starve/die from this, but production stalls and you can't train — another reason to hold food as items and open on demand.

## Strategy for mass T1 training
- Hold food as items, open just before/while training, stack **resource-production buffs**, **gather** high-level tiles, and use **alliance resource help**.

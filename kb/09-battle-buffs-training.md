# Evony — Battle Mechanics for Troop Training & Buff Suggestions

## Which troops to train (by purpose)
- **T1 (lowest tier)** — meat-shield / layers; trained in huge numbers (this bot's target). Absorbs losses so high tiers survive rounds. **T1 mounted** is the "trap keep" meta — with strong buffs/debuffs, T1 mounted tanks and dishes damage before falling.
- **Mounted** — best vs **monsters/bosses** (highest attack); send **single-tier, mounted-only** for no-loss kills.
- **Highest available tier** — the damage core of PvP marches, layered with all lower tiers.
- **Boss tier gates:** L2 boss ≥**T5**; **event bosses ≥T10**.
- **Layering:** field every class across all tiers so cheap low-tiers eat enemy rounds.

## Buff/debuff prioritization (what to stack)
- **Research:** prioritize **Military** (troop attack/def/HP, training speed, march size) and **Advancement** (economy/growth) first.
- **General gear:** raise general attributes; craft **Ares** or **Achaemenidae** equipment and refine. A full **6-piece Achaemenidae set = −15% debuff**.
- **Sub-city generals:** their *buffs* only help that sub-city's troops (irrelevant to the main keep), but their **debuffs affect ALL your keep's troops** — so equip sub-city generals purely for **debuffs**.
- **Defense debuff order:** cut enemy **ground HP + defense** first, then **archer def/attack**, then **mounted HP/attack**.

## Buff sources (stack multiplicatively)
Generals (main skill + specialties + skill books) · Academy/Military tech · Blazons · Spiritual Beasts/Dragons · Subordinate Cities · VIP · Monarch Gear (**War Horn** = march size) · Art Hall artworks · buff items.

## Implications for the training bot
- The bot trains **T1 Warriors (ground)** — cheapest meat-shield. To optimize for **combat power / trap-keep meta**, switch to **T1 mounted** (same bot flow, different troop tab/building).
- **Training-capacity** and **training-speed** buffs (Army Camp, Military research, VIP) raise batch size (currently **269,228**) and cut batch time — the direct levers to make each bot cycle produce more.

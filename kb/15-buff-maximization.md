# Buff Maximization (VIP23, Keep 40s — economy-first)

Every buff lever in ETKR and how to prioritize it for an account that bulk-trains ground
troops (economy first, account power second). Buffs come from ~11 stacking systems.

> Confidence: qualitative strategy and the systems below are cross-checked across theria,
> onechilledgamer, evonyguru, evonytkrguide, evonybuilds. Some exact per-level/per-tier %s are
> unverified (evonyguidewiki/Fandom blocked automated fetch) — flagged inline. See the
> uncertainty + myths list at the bottom before spending big.

## Two mental models that decide everything

1. **Buffs stack additively** across research + gear + generals + sub-cities + beast seals +
   monarch + VIP + rank + alliance + titles + consumables. Same-name timed consumables refresh
   the timer, they don't double.
2. **General buffs are SLOT-dependent** — the single most-missed rule:
   - **Leading a march:** everything applies (base stats, level, ascension, specialties, skill
     books, gear, dragon, beast). The *only* slot where gathering/load/march and dragon/beast
     buffs fire.
   - **Assistant:** only skill books + specialties + special skill (no stats/ascension/gear/
     dragon/beast).
   - **Sub-city Mayor:** only Leadership & Politics (+ sub-city skill books) → construction/
     training/gold. Historic Mayor unlocks up to 4 buffs; Lead+Pol ≥900 activates War Tactics.
   - **Duty Officer:** a building-tied, city-wide buff gated by the general's stats.

Economy buffs and combat buffs live in **different systems** — don't expect a combat set to
help gathering, or vice-versa.

## Economy buff sources (magnitudes)

- **Duty Officers** — best buff-per-effort, permanent, cheap (needs Lv18+/4★ generals). Keep
  **+20% construction**, Academy **+20% research**, Barracks **+20% ground training +5% ground
  stats** (Guan Yu), Stables/Archer/Workshop +20% their type, Hospital **+25% cap**. **Assign
  BEFORE starting a task** — assigning after does nothing.
- **Academy — Advancement (economy) tree** (base+Adv+Super, additive): Gathering **≈+180%/
  resource** + load +80% + gather-march-speed +80%; Construction **≈+180% + Builder = +2 build
  queues**; Research **≈+170%**; Production **≈+170%/building**; Training **≈+170% + 400
  capacity** (training research is in the **Military** tree, not Advancement).
- **Subordinate cities** — 7 cultures × 2 buffs, scaling Common→Epic. For a trainer stack
  **China** (Resource Production +4→20%, **Training Speed +2→10%**) and **Korea** (Warehouse
  +6→30%, **Extra Gather Resources +3→15%**). Same-culture stacks additively: **9 Korean Epics
  ≈ +135% gather yield**; 9 China Epics ≈ +90% training speed. Cap scales with **Prestige Rank**
  (push it). Historic Mayor (Lead+Pol ≥900) w/ training skill books: Charles Martel (+50%
  construction/+30% training), Catherine II/Uesugi (+35% training). **No sub-city training-COST
  reduction exists** — only speed/production.
- **Monarch gear** (6 swappable typed pieces): **Strength Crown** during builds, **Martial
  Crown** during training, typed **Crystal** (Light=food/Wood=lumber/Thunder=stone/Golden=ore)
  + **Loyalty Horn** (load/march) the whole time a march sits on a tile. Swap per activity.
- **Monarch talents** (1 pt/level, growth build): Free Construction, Resource Production,
  Research Speed, **Gathering Boost +20% (confirmed)**, Training Speed. Two saveable builds.
- **Spiritual Beast Seals** — passive, **no attachment to a general needed** (this is the key):
  beast Lv10 → Pasture Lv30, seals to Lv10 give account-wide **gathering +20–25%, construction
  +12.5–15%, training +30–35%, production up to +92.5%**. Best economy beast: Pyr Dragon. One of
  the best passive economy investments; costs no dragon slot.
- **VIP23 (yours, permanent):** Construction **+39%**, Research **+36%**, Training **+40%**,
  Healing +57%, March +42%, Resource **Production +47%**, +70 general cap, +1 march slot. (VIP is
  production, not tile-gather speed, and does not itself grant a 2nd build queue.)
- **Prestige Rank:** **Duke = +30% training/healing/march + 5 sub-city slots**; Archduke +50%/+6;
  Regent +70%. Big passive training + more sub slots.
- **General (military) gear = where economy gear buffs live:** **Champion's** 4-pc +10% gather /
  full +10% extra resources; **King's** (resource-type pieces — wear marching, swap to Champion's
  once gathering starts); Transcendence +8%; **Refine** slots (gather speed / load / construction
  / training / gold — **not research**), ~15–25%/line. Civilization gear is **combat only**.
- **Alliance:** Science **Agriculture/Lumbering/Masonry/Mining +40% each (Super +60%)** on
  alliance tiles; Transportation +100% capacity; Alliance Help throughput (Embassy + research)
  shaves construction/research/training time. Victory Column banner **+25% construction** in zone.
- **King titles** (if alliance holds throne): Craftsman +15% construction, Scholar +15%
  research, Quartermaster +10% gather / −10% upkeep. **Verify in-client — community reports some
  historically didn't apply.**
- **Timed consumables:** **Gathering Speed +50% (8h / 24h)** — keep one rolling. Combat
  Atk/Def/HP boosts are 1h. Standalone timed construction/training % items are not confirmed —
  those accelerations come from the systems above.

## Prioritized action list (economy first)

**Tier S — do first (cheap, permanent, high %):**
1. **Fill every Duty Officer slot** (Keep/Barracks/Academy/Hospital…). Near-zero cost, always on.
2. **Drive Academy Advancement economy research:** Construction (+Builder = 2 queues) →
   Typography/Super (research speed) → Production line → Gathering+Load → Conscription training.
3. **Typed Monarch gear per activity** (Strength/Martial Crown, typed Crystal + Loyalty Horn on tile).

**Tier A — high value, moderate effort:**
4. **Stack China + Korea sub-cities** with Historic Mayors (Lead+Pol ≥900 + training books).
5. **Push Prestige Rank → Duke → Archduke** (+30/50% training, +5/6 sub slots).
6. **Grow Spiritual Beast Seals** (passive account-wide economy %).
7. **Run a growth Monarch talent build** (construction/research/gathering/training).

**Tier B — grind / situational:**
8. **Gathering gear + refines** (Champion's + King's + Transcendence; refine gather/load/train
   lines) on cheap *leading* gather generals (Princess Lucy, Gaius Marius, Attila/Amir Timur for
   load).
9. **Strong alliance** for gather tech (+40–60%), Victory Column (+25% construction), Help throughput.
10. **King titles** if your alliance holds the throne (verify they apply).
11. **Keep a 24h gathering +50% rolling**; stockpile speed-ups for SvS Training/Development stages.

**Combat / account-power track (secondary):**
- **Blazons** (biggest combat lever): 2 Atk + 2 Def + 2 HP per troop type, level to 15–20
  (~+100% each per type) — prioritize Ground. Combat-only (no economy).
- **Civ gear Star Atlas** — owned-set passives (~+20%) apply even unequipped (Heian/Romulus/Koryo).
- **Dragon to Lv10** (never-die) then level/refine; assign to PvP *lead* generals (not Duty).
- **Ascend + specialties + Lv4 skill books** on main PvP leads (ascension/dragon fire only when leading).
- **Council of State** appointments + Military research; higher VIP if you spend.

## Bot-relevant notes

- **Passive buffs never need reactivation:** research, sub-cities, beast seals, VIP, rank, duty
  officers, monarch talents. Set-and-forget — invest here first.
- **Only a few TIMED items need periodic re-tapping:** gathering +50% (8h/24h) and combat
  boosts. Automatable by tapping stable inventory positions; avoid churning the 1h beast/dragon
  swap cooldown. (ADB reactivation specifics are undocumented publicly — inference.)
- Economy buffs here directly multiply the **auto-gather** yield (kb/14) and **training**
  throughput; Duty-Officer + research + China/Korea subs are the compounding base.

## Uncertainty register (don't treat as fixed)

Exact per-level general stat gains; exact %/tier for specialties and per-star special-skill
gains; exact %/level for monarch gear and most talents (only Gathering Boost +20% confirmed);
sub-city cap (sources give 9/11/12); Military Academy per-formation %s; Temple/Wonder
magnitudes; whether all King titles apply. Some blazon / max-gather figures are via search
summaries (host blocked) — directional, not verified.

**Confirmed myths to ignore:** ETKR has **no** gear gem-sockets, **no** gear "enhancement
stones", **no** "Sicilian" gathering gear, **no** cross-account buff broadcasting ("buff city"
is just sub-city stacking or a resource alt), **no** sub-city training-*cost* reduction, and
**no** "Aegis/Viridescent" dragons.

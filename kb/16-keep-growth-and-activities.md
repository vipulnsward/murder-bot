# Keep Growth & Activities (VIP23, Keep 40s, bot-assisted)

What actually moves a high-level account, and which repeatable activities are worth a bot's
time. Two levers dominate: **Academy research** (permanent buff multipliers) and **troop
count/tier**; building levels are a distant third that mostly *unlock* research and troop tiers.

> Numeric caveat: `evonyguidewiki.com` was unreachable and Fandom rate-limited during
> research, so several figures are single-sourced from search snippets. Keep-40 resource costs
> came back internally inconsistent — **treat exact Keep-40s resource numbers as unverified**.
> The qualitative strategy is corroborated across multiple guides.

## Keep upgrade path past 40 — gated by monuments, not resources

Keep 41–45 want roughly **50–90B of *each* basic resource** (food/lumber/stone/ore). But the
real bottleneck is the **monument/prerequisite buildings** and the **power + generals** to
raise them:

- **Victory Column** — unlocks Keep 36; L1 needs ~2 strong (~5.8M-power) generals.
- **Military Academy** — unlocks Keep 36; **Keep 37 needs Mil.Academy L1, Keep 40 needs L4**.
- **Holy Palace** — tied to the Keep-38 step.
- **Triumphal Arch** — buildable Keep 38; **Keep 39 requires Triumphal Arch L1**.
- **Keep 45 requires all army buildings at L44** (Barracks, Archer Camp, Stables, Workshop…).

There is **no separate "refined resource" keep-upgrade material** in ETKR — keeps use the four
standard resources only. The advanced-material grinds (below) are for generals/gear, not the
Keep. Stockpile the 50–90B as **boxed** resource items and unbox only at the instant of
upgrade (boxed = un-plunderable). Sources: evonyguidewiki keep-upgrade, simplegameguide,
Pillar of Gaming.

## Building priorities

1. **Academy = always #1.** Research is where account power and buffs come from. Prioritize the
   **Advancement tree**: Construction / Adv / Super Construction (build speed), Typography /
   Super Typography (research speed), plus troop attack/def/HP trees.
2. **Troop buildings** (Barracks/Archer Camp/Stables/Workshop) — raise to unlock higher tiers +
   training capacity. Archer Camp/Tower is the usual power/defense priority.
3. **Wall + Trap Factory** — first line of defense; upgrade before Keep/Archer Towers. Traps are
   consumed when attacked → build ahead of PvP (SvS), not as permanent power.
4. **Rally Spot** — keep at the **same level as the Keep** for full rally size.
5. **Utility** (Warehouse/Hospital/Watchtower) — survivability, not power: Warehouse protects
   resources, Hospital sets wounded capacity, Watchtower gives incoming-attack intel.
6. **Resource buildings** — low priority; boxed event/boss/gather resources dwarf field output.

## Where power comes from (efficient order)

**Research (biggest permanent multiplier) → train troops continuously → develop
generals/specialties → monarch gear & sub-cities → building levels as they gate the above.**

## Construction & research speed

- **Second construction queue** is the biggest accelerator — a permanent one from **Academy
  research**, else the VIP builder rents at **300 gems / 3 days**. ≈ doubles throughput.
- **Duty-officer generals — assign BEFORE starting the task** (assigning after does nothing;
  ~+20% from the basic skill). Construction: Meshchersky / Zhuge Liang / Skanderbeg. Research:
  Sun Wu / Hammurabi / Eulji Mundeok.
- **Alliance Help** — each member's tap shaves time off every queued build/research; stacks.
- **Stacking rule:** use category-specific (Construction/Research) speedups first, save
  "General" speedups for emergencies, and land big upgrades when alliance build-speed buff +
  monarch gear + **event bonuses** (Consuming Return / Monarch-Competition City-Development)
  are all active.

## Advanced materials (for generals/gear, NOT the keep)

- **Runestones** — open general **specialties**; **12,800 of each of 4 types to max one
  general** (months). Only on top gold epic historicals. Farm: Pan boss, Delphic Oracle,
  gather/kill events, return events.
- **Refining Stones** — general **gear refining**; extremely scarce ("most valuable item").
  Don't perfect-refine gear you'll replace.

## Activities & events — value-per-effort, with automation tags

Tags: **[BOT]** bots automate it · **[semi]** action automates, claim may be manual · **[MAN]**.

**Daily musts**
- **145 daily activity points** (Tavern) — final chest can drop **Key of Conscription → epic
  gold-general fragment**. Falls out of gathering/hunting/building/training. **[semi]**
- **Gather on all march slots** — resources + activity/event points. **[BOT]**
- **Alliance Help** every login + **Alliance Science donation** (stop before it wants gems, ~4h
  refresh). **[BOT/semi]**
- **Wall Patrol** 3 free/day (gold, material chest, monarch gear); **Bounty Quests** 8/day;
  **Daily Quests** ~200 gems/day; **free Tax / free Levy / Warehouse collect**; **Shrine daily
  + offerings**. **[BOT/semi]**
- **Wheel of Fortune** — save to the 900-coin pull; **VIP daily chest**; **Monarch talent
  point** (take "Offer" first: +20% reputation & Lord EXP). **[MAN]**

**Recurring events (time pushes to these)**
- **Server vs Server (SvS)** — biweekly. **Server Monarch Competition** stages (Gathering,
  Power, City Development, Troop Training, Monster Killing, Kill Event) — **do build/research/
  train/kill inside the matching stage so the action scores twice.**
- **Consuming Return** (~2-week cycle) — resources spent on construction pay back speedups/
  materials/stamina; stockpile speedups the week before, deploy during the event.
- **World Boss** (weekend, 48h) — **5 free hits/week**, can't lose, ~**500+ gems/week** + rare
  mats. **Boss Monster Event** doubles spawn rate = best speedup farm.
- **Battlefield (Clash of Civilizations)** — gems, honor, speedups, research stones, premium
  generals.

**Monster / boss rallies** — spend stamina on monsters; join alliance boss rallies. High-boss
alliance sessions can clear **~5 days of timers** at once (training/healing speedups +
materials). Send only highest-attack tier (usually mounted) so bosses die in one round.
**Fully automatable** — auto-find, auto-start, auto-join (with filters), auto-attack. **[BOT]**

## What to avoid (traps)

- **Never unbox resources until the moment you upgrade** — unboxed feeds troop upkeep and makes
  you a plunder target; boxed is untouchable. #1 defensive rule for a big keep.
- **Don't trade gems for resources/gold** (store is wildly overpriced). Reserve gems for gear
  refining and dragons (~300k gems).
- **Don't spend runestones / red skill books on purple/blue generals** — save for top golds.
- **Don't skip 145 activity points or the world-boss free hits** — free general frags + gems.
- **Hide gear/VIP/march routes** in Options so enemies can't scout you.
- **SvS exposure:** unshielded + unboxed resources + un-hospitalized army = getting "zeroed."
  **An auto-shield-on-incoming-attack bot is the main safeguard** — verify it before leaving
  unattended.

## Prioritized repeatables (VIP23, Keep 40s)

1. Both **construction queues + Academy research** running 24/7, duty officers pre-assigned,
   timed to buffs. **[semi]** — biggest long-term power.
2. **Gather non-stop** on all marches. **[BOT]**
3. **Train troops continuously**, pushing in Monarch-Competition / Consuming-Return windows. **[semi]**
4. **Alliance boss + monster rallies** (auto-join/auto-start) 24/7. **[BOT]** — days of speedups.
5. **Auto-shield on incoming attack + keep resources boxed.** **[BOT]** — protects everything.
6. **145 daily activity points** (falls out of 2–4). **[semi]**
7. **Alliance Help + daily Science donation.** **[BOT/semi]**
8. **World Boss 5 free hits/weekend.** **[semi]**
9. Daily freebies (wall patrol, tax, levy, warehouse, shrine, bounty, daily quests). **[BOT/semi]**
10. Wheel of Fortune (900-coin pull) + VIP/alliance gift chests. **[MAN]**
